Floor planer

Floor Planner is an interactive Flash AS3 application designed for creating and visualizing floor and wall painting plans. It serves as a tool for users to design and customize interior spaces by experimenting with various floor and wall colors or patterns.

1. Floor and Wall Design:

Users can select from predefined floor types and wall paint colors to visualize different design options. Offers a variety of textures, colors, and patterns for walls and floors.

2. Photo Customization:

Users can upload their own room photos to experiment with designs in a personalized context. Alternatively, the application provides sample photos of rooms for designing and testing ideas.

3. Interactive Editing:

The application uses an intuitive interface for selecting, applying, and previewing changes in real-time. Includes drag-and-drop functionality for easy adjustments and navigation.

4. Customization Flexibility:

Allows users to create and save multiple designs to compare options. Offers tools to adjust the brightness, contrast, and textures to suit user preferences.

5. Export and Sharing:

Users can save or print their final designs. Integration for sharing designs via email or social platforms.
